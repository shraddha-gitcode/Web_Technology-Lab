MY STORY

My Reading Journey

Books became more than paper and ink for me; they became sanctuaries, secret confidants, and silent companions. From the tender days of tenth grade, when motivational reads lit a fire in my spirit, they whispered courage into my veins and urged me to chase brilliance in studies, to dream of medicine, to believe in the impossible. Then, like fate disguised as friendship, a gentle nudge from a friend carried me beyond the realm of self-help into the boundless skies of fiction. The very first spell was cast by Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone, a doorway to magic, wonder, and endless adventures that refused to end when the last page closed. That single book became a key, unlocking worlds where imagination soared higher than ambition, where stories stitched themselves into the fabric of my soul. Since then, my shelves have blossomed into gardens of wisdom and wonder. Among them rests The Forty Rules of Love, a book that does not merely sit‚Äîit breathes, it sings, it teaches the heart to listen to silence and the soul to dance with eternity. Each title I've held close is more than a possession; it is a chapter of my journey, a mirror of my thoughts, a vessel of my dreams. And now, as I pass on my pre-loved books, I am not merely giving away bound pages. I am offering fragments of myself‚Äîmy highlights, my scribbled notes in the margins, my laughter tucked between paragraphs, my tears pressed into paper fibers. These books carry my footprints, my echoes, my imagination. So let us connect, not just as readers but as wanderers of worlds. Let us swap stories, trade dreams, and weave together a tapestry of shared adventures.
